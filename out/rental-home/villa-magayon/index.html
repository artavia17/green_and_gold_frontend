Picture this: the perfect match between luxury and adventure. A hidden jewel, surrounded by the deepest, most vibrant green you’ve ever seen. It may feel like a dream, but it’s actually a very real place. Discover Casa Magayon, the home of your next adventure.

This peaceful sanctuary is located in Península Papagayo, situated inside a luxury resort community designed to cater to all of your senses.

The beaches’ amazing views and ocean waves will invite you to discover them first hand. You’ll have plenty of opportunities too, with 31 different beaches waiting for you, several of them just 2 to 5 minutes away.

In the morning, let the beautiful and varied Costa Rican biodiversity surprise you; the trees provide a home for a vast array of new friends like howler monkeys, butterflies, and hummingbirds just to name a few. Meanwhile, the night will bring the relaxing sound of chirping crickets to soothe you to sleep.

Satisfy your adventurous side by swimming, kayaking or paddleboarding undisturbed thanks to the natural pathway offering private access to the beach and finish your day unwinding at the spa. Or if you prefer; cool off at the private beach club, take your golf clubs for a spin or play some sets in one of your new favorite Tennis courts after a relaxing day in the Marina.

Your Dream Vacation Home

The five fully furnished rooms offer the perfect accommodation for up to 10 people, each with a private bathroom.

The master bedroom features a comfortable king bed and a private studio; and the other guests will find a pleasant sleep, thanks to the 3 rooms furnished with a queen bed and the double twin beds on the last one.

During your stay, you’ll be able to enjoy the different spaces inside the house, such as the two fully equipped kitchens and dining areas, the spacious cinema room, the bbq area, and the breathtaking views from the balcony. And of course, you’ll feel right at home with a full-sized garage and a practical laundry room.

Amenities you’ll love

We know you’ll love your stay both inside and outside Casa Magayón. Your vacation will be filled with enjoyment thanks to several thoughtfully planned activities:

Relax at the infinity pool, perfectly heated by the amazing tropical sun.

Golf like a pro in the delightfully tropical Arnold Palmer golf course or let your competitive side fly at one of the four tennis courts.

Enjoy a delicious dinner at the private beach club, right after a relaxing day in the Marina.

Or just, let yourself feel at paradise with a visit to the full-service spa.
