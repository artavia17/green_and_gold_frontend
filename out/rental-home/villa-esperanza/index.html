“Waking up in Paradise” This is how it has been described by those staying in Villa Esperanza in Peninsula Papagayo. You’ll hear monkeys and a chorus of tropical birds and the continuous rolling surf! You can see the beautiful Pacific Ocean from every room in Villa Esperanza. It truly is a paradise.

Located near the Four Seasons Hotel and overlooking Prieta Beach and the Pacific Ocean, Villa Esperanza (House of Hope) offers all the amenities you want in a luxury vacation home. Each bedroom is its own colorful, yet private retreat – with a bathroom, bathrobe and the what you are expecting on a get-away vacation.  In the Master Suite, you will live in your own luxurious spa-like atmosphere, where you have yet another stunning ocean view, a large marble shower and an outdoor garden shower open to Costa Rican skies. You will be awakened by the aroma of Costa Rican coffee at the coffee bar, then enjoy the custom breakfast (including the local Tico favorites and delicious tropical fruits!) prepared for you by the resident couple. There is light housekeeping service provided and a laundry room for your use.

You can pass the time relaxing by the infinity pool in peaceful surroundings, grab a chair in the quiet niche to read a book or join in the unending activities at Prieta Beach Club. Only a short walk away, Prieta offers many water sports and activities. Also included are programs for kids and teens as well as the electric bicycles that can be used around the Peninsula! The Nature Center offers a wide variety of educational and fun activities, too.

Villa Esperanza allows plenty of space indoors to enjoy a family meal, watch a movie or play games. There is a large covered outdoor area poolside to stay cool during the day as you barbeque on the grill. At night, take in the Costa Rican nature sounds around the glow-in-the-dark-tiled pool where you can entertain your entire group under millions of stars.

Should you want to venture outside of the Peninsula Papagayo gates, our Concierge will assist you in finding any type of restaurant, activity or need you may have. If fishing or boating is enticing, Villa Esperanza has a private boat for your use for a nominal charge, offering the opportunity to see whales, dolphins and gorgeous sunsets.

It will be a vacation you will never forget.
